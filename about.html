this is an internet art project where people can not only submit euphemisms and translations, but also find connections between words and connect with each other. maybe net art can encourage us to be more critical about euphemistic language and more reflective about the way we communicate. the project was conceived and is currently overseen by a university student studying media and culture. it will change as more people contribute to it.
